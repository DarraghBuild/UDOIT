Module 0: Your Course Introduction

Introduction

In this module, you will become acquainted with the resources you have at your disposal and become familiarized with the workings of this online course. The purpose of this module is to help you become familiar with Webcourses@UCF and to learn the routine, expectations, and organization of the course.

Objectives

By the end of this module, you should be able to:

  • Navigate your way around this Webcourse.
  • Locate course materials including the syllabus, schedule, and weekly content.
  • Know how to take an online quiz (by completing the Webcourses@UCF orientation).
  • Know how to use the discussions tool of this course.
  • Successfully connect your computer to the UCF library network and access library resources.
  • Know that your browser is compatible with Webcourses@UCF.

Webcourses@UCF Student Orientation

  1. Click on the Webcourses@UCF Orientation on the course home page and complete the orientation.
  2. After completing the Webcourses@UCF Orientation, take the Student Orientation Quiz located in the Assessments tool.

Discussion Posting: Introduction

  1. Select the “Discussions” link in the Course Tools menu.
  2. In the "Introduction” topic, post a brief message introducing yourself to your classmates.

Off-Campus Access Library Tutorial

  1. Go to http://library.ucf.edu/Reference/Videos/Common/Overview/Overview.asp

  3. Follow the link to this article. You should be able to view the entire article and download it as a pdf file.
