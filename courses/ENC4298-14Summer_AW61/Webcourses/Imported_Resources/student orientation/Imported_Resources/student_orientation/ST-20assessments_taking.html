Taking Assessments

After you select Begin Assessment from the assessment information screen, the assessment appears in a pop-up window and, if applicable, the timer for the assessment is started.

Each question provides a space to enter your answer. For each answer, you must select Save Answer. Each time you save an answer, the question status is updated in the right-hand frame; answered questions have a check mark. The right-hand frame also displays the amount of time you have left to complete the assessment.

Once you have entered and saved all your answers, select Finish. A confirmation message appears. After you have verified that you have answered all of the questions, the assessment is submitted.

To ensure that you have saved all your answers before submitting the assessment, click Save All before you click Finish.

After submitting an assessment, you can view your results. Let's look at how to view assessment results and statistics next.
